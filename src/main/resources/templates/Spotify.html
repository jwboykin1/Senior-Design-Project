Answers: Select the Portion of the E-mail you Would Like to Check!

Answer for Top Portion

There are a couple places where this logo just seems off. If you look at the
resolution it is much worse than a real logo should be. Also the Copyright
is so small it is almost unreadable! A real logo is shown below.

This is what the actual Spotify Logo looks like and while there are different color palettes
you can tell this one is more legitimate from a glance.

Answer for Header Portion

The email begins with "Importan Spotify Notification!" This seems somewhat legitimate
until you think about how broad that is, that could mean a number of different topics
pertaining to your account. A legitimate company like Spotify would use a more specific
account notice. Something like "An Important Notice Regarding your Premium Status".
That would be much more believable than what is provided here.

Answer for Middle Portion

This portion begins with the line that phishers try to use quite often "need to validate some account information" any
reputable company will never and I mean never ask you to validate your account credentials! That information is private
to yourself and wherever it is stored on the company's database. This should be a dead giveaway of a phishing scam so be
sure to remember this! The link also when hovered upon takes you to a Youtube domain.

This small section is highlighted because of the fact that it is not bolded or italicized or have a larger font size Many
companies use tactics like those mentioned to make their teams stand out more from the email for professionalism.

Answer for Bottom Portion

The logo is the same as the one pictured in the top with another unreadable Copyright.

Phishing scams often throw in portions like this at the bottom to make it look more legitimate. Do not fall for such traps!

The final section shows that this email was sent to a certain user. In a lot of phishing attempts phishers make mistakes at
areas like this because they send the phishing email out to so many users. Be sure to take note of this area!
